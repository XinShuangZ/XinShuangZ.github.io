About Me

Currently I am a M.Sc. student of School of Intelligence Science and Technology in Nanjing University Suzhou Campus and a member of LAMDA Group, led by professor Zhi-Hua Zhou.

I received my B.Sc. degree in School of Mathematics and Statistics from Central South University. In 2023, I was admitted to study for a M.Sc. degree in Nanjing University. I am currently working as a Data Scientist Intern at Microsoft STCA in Suzhou.

Research Interests

My research interests include Machine Learning. Currently, I am focusing on the sub-fields:

  • Graph Neuro-Symbolic Learning: Integrating machine learning and logical reasoning within graph structures, such as graph neural networks, probabilistic graphical models, and causal graphs, with a particular focus on applications in biological metabolic networks and content delivery networks.

  • Reasoning in LLMs: Improving the logical reasoning ability of large language models, with a particular focus on applications in fake news detection.
